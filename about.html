About the Club

Our Story

Adam Banerjee founded Rogue Lantern after seeing the Summerlin community grow rapidly and recognizing the need for a premium, intimate comedy room in the area.

After more than a decade working across comedy clubs, he built Rogue Lantern as a place where established comedians can workshop new material in front of intelligent audiences, and where emerging comics can earn meaningful opportunities on a respected stage.

Rogue Lantern is designed to create real local community through live performance, while maintaining the standards of a top-tier room with a 90-seat format, strong curation, and a consistent guest experience.

Rogue Lantern • Established 2026 • Summerlin, Las Vegas
